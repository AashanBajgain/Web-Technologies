Details Of Thomas J. Watson Sr.

Thomas Watson was the CEO and chairman of IBM from 1914 to 1956. He developed the company's culture and management style after working with NCR's John Henry Patterson.He transformed IBM into a successful organization by developing a culture and management style that revolved around the use of punch card tabulating machines. Watson was a self-made billionaire who was regarded as the world's greatest salesman before he died in 1956.
